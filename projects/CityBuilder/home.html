City Builder



This is a City Builder where you can build and contain your small town. You are starting with an empty map.

!!The recommended Display Resolution is ≥ 1280 x 1024!!

Use F11 to enter the fullscreen mode to get the best result. Make sure that you use zoom level 100% in your Browser. Adjust the zoom when using a different resolution so nothing is hidden.
